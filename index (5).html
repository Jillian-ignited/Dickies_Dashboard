Dickies peaked at $208M in 2019 but has declined steadily to $83.6M in FY 2024. FY 2026 wholesale shipments are projected at $75.3M (actual + projected). The opportunity: Reverse the decline and surpass the 2019 peak.

The Path Forward: Exit underperforming SKUs (reduce Walmart's inventory burden), optimize core assortment (improve their turns), focus on winners (increase velocity). This drives the $14.0M wholesale growth opportunity: faster turns more frequent reorders more shipments from Dickies to Walmart.
